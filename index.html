About Mika Bot

Mika is a bot designed to bring fun and functionality to your Discord server. With interactive commands, such as playing Rock, Paper, Scissors or asking the 8ball, Mika is the perfect companion to keep your conversations lively!

The bot provides several features, from simple fun commands to more engaging interactions, making it great for moderators and members alike.

Bot Commands

/ping

Use this command to check the bot's latency. The bot will respond with the response time in milliseconds.

/ppt

Play Rock, Paper, Scissors with Mika. Choose one of the options, and the bot will make its move automatically.

/coinflip

Flip a coin with Mika. The bot randomly selects either Heads or Tails.

/8ball

Ask the 8ball a question, and Mika will give you a random answer. Perfect when you're unsure what to do!

/piadas

Ask Mika for a joke and enjoy a funny response generated randomly by the bot.

How Mika Bot Was Created

Mika was developed using the Python programming language and the discord.py library, which simplifies the creation of bots for Discord.

The bot is designed to be easy to use, while also providing a variety of commands that make it both useful and fun. Slash commands are used for smoother interaction with users.

Additionally, Mika features Rich Presence, which shows the bot's status, and integration with the Discord API for a seamless and responsive experience.
